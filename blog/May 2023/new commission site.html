New Commission Site

Welcome to my newest website! Great things will begin here!

May 26, 2023

Up Until now

This is my latest and greatest (hopefully forever as I update it) website. Up until this point, I had been learning web development tips and tricks from around the internet through YouTube channels such as Web Dev Simplified and Coder Coder as well as websites such as W3Schools and Stack Overflow. This site was built on all of that knowledge and the latest technologies in web dev.

Bootstrap 5, CSS 3, and HTML 5

As I learned more advanced styling techniques through working on a combination of personal projects such as my portfolio website and some super secret group projects, I began to take commissions for video editing and portfolio websites. Great friends suggested that I look into productizing my services and forwarding commission requests to a website where my services and rates are clearly described.

Here is the result of that feedback.

The Future

This commission site will be the hub of my website and video editing services moving forward. Ideally, I will fully integrate a webstore into the site for the simplest user experience possible. This will need some time to implement. My intention is to receive commissions through my contact form or direct emails until I complete the store integration of the site.

Expect to see this site become more alive with deeper interactivity, more blog posts, more services, and more content!
